Use case 1

Ebiz Airlines

Ebiz Airlines is a customer centric company and leverage technology innovation to provides exceptional service through out the customer journeys.


Problem
  • Need a quick & flexible way to engage customers 24/7
  • Booking, payments, processes & check-ins are too complicated leads to bad user experiences
Solution
  • We build an Airline chatbot to unified all the process into one single contact point.
  • Leverage App Cloud - Heroku & Salesforce CRM to provide user's best experience with both automated messages and human support.
Result
  • Customers could find available flights, book tickets, check-in, boarding, change seat and update flight status all in one single app they already know & love: Facebook Messenger.
  • The customer can contact Ebiz social support team 24/7 through Messenger.

Use case 2

Ebiz Airlines

Ebiz Airlines is a customer centric company and leverage technology innovation to provides exceptional service through out the customer journeys.


Problem
  • Customer service could not response fast enough to support passengers
  • Customer experience with Airlines is not just flight or tickets. Need to expand customer touchpoint to increase brand awareness and collect customer behaviors
Solution
  • We upgrade the Airline chatbot to Artificial Intelligent using Wit.AI to learn and response like 'human' at mass scale, 24/7 365 days.
  • Heroku connects Salesforce CRM, Service Cloud, use Wit.AI to help Chatbot delivers smart, revolutionize customer experience
Result
  • Customer get answers fast, instant and completely satisfied with Airlines customer service.
  • Passengers could ask for Chatbot help anytime, anywhere with more than 100 languages. They feel Airline chatbot is their smart, fast & trusted travel advisor.
