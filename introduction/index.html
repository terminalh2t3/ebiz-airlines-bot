Overview

Airline chatbot for Facebook Messenger that could help customers find available flights, online bookings, check-in, boarding pass, change seats and update flight status.

A flexible – user friendly customer support solution across multiple touchpoints & real time messaging with passengers.

  • Booking confirmation
  • Ticket payment via messenger
  • Check-in, boarding pass
  • Change seats, extra luggage
  • Flight status update

Travel Assistance

Personalize 1-1 customer experience using semi or full auto-response 24/7/365 with Chatbot for Airlines – AI version.

Turns Chatbot into a personal travel assistant that helps passengers looking for hotels, restaurants or tour guide using their local language – be a useful friend, creating a journey together.

  • 24/7 social customer service
  • Hybrid or full auto response message
  • Nature language processing with Wit.ai
  • Search hotels, nearby places
  • Personal travel assistant
